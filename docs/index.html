Overview

One ledger. Every document flows through it.

Every financial event — an invoice, a bill, a payment, depreciation, a POS day-end — is mapped to a balanced journal entry and posted through a single chokepoint that checks the period, enforces approval, writes an audit trail, and can never be quietly edited afterwards. Corrections are reversals, not edits. That is what makes the books trustworthy.

A live trial balance for the demo company — balanced to the centavo, exportable to PDF or XLSX.

01 — Feature reference

Everything Apex does

A complete tour of the modules, grouped the way the app's navigation is.

01 · Ledger

Double-entry ledger & the posting chokepoint

The heart of Apex. A hierarchical chart of accounts and an immutable journal where every posted entry is balanced, period-checked and audited.

  • Chart of accounts with normal-balance & subtype
  • Draft → Submit → Approve → Post workflow (optional approval gate)
  • Posted entries are immutable; corrections are reversals
  • Accounting periods & fiscal-year open/close
  • Manual journal entries with departments, projects, funds, branches
02 · Receivables

Accounts Receivable

Invoice customers with proper VAT segregation, then track every peso until it's collected.

  • Sales invoices: VATable / exempt / zero-rated per line
  • Credit memos & application to invoices
  • Customer payments with allocation
  • AR aging, statements of account, dunning letters
03 · Payables

Accounts Payable

Vendor bills with the three-bucket input-VAT attribution the BIR expects, plus expanded withholding.

  • Input VAT routed per line: direct-vatable, exempt, or common
  • Expanded withholding tax (EWT) on payments
  • Vendor payments & debit memos
  • AP aging and outstanding balances
04 · Banking

Banking & reconciliation

Import statements, match them to the ledger, and prove the bank balance.

  • CSV bank-statement import with auto-match suggestions
  • Reconciliation that only closes at a zero difference
  • Deposits, transfers and bank charges
  • Per-account currency
05 · Inventory

Inventory & COGS

Track stock items and let cost of goods sold post automatically.

  • Goods & service items with pricing modes
  • Weighted-average cost of goods sold
  • Inventory adjustments for count variances
  • Optional negative-stock guard
06 · Fixed assets

Fixed assets & depreciation

Capitalize assets and run depreciation on a schedule.

  • Asset categories with useful life & rates
  • Place-in-service capitalization
  • Monthly depreciation runs
  • Disposal with automatic gain / loss
07 · Multi-currency

Foreign currency & FX revaluation

Transact in foreign currencies and surface the gains and losses correctly.

  • Foreign-currency invoices & bills at a document rate
  • Historical spot-rate table
  • Month-end unrealized FX revaluation working paper
  • Realized gain / loss on settlement
08 · Tax

Tax & BIR compliance

Prepare and persist the quarterly and annual returns, with the supporting exports.

  • 2550Q VAT · 1601EQ EWT · 2551Q percentage tax
  • 1702Q corporate & 1701Q individual income tax
  • Alphalist (QAP), SLSP.DAT, and 2307 certificates
  • Immutable filed snapshots with PDF facsimiles
09 · Reports

Financial & statutory reports

Twenty-plus reports, each filterable by date and exportable to PDF or XLSX.

  • Trial balance, balance sheet, P&L (+ comparatives)
  • Cash flow, changes in equity, general ledger & journal
  • Books of accounts: Sales, Purchase, Cash Receipts, Cash Disbursements
  • AR/AP aging, VAT & EWT summaries, budget vs actual
10 · Automation

Recurring transactions

Set up invoices, bills or journal entries that repeat, and run the ones that are due.

  • Recurring invoices, bills & journal entries
  • Monthly, quarterly or annual schedules
  • Run-due execution with an audit trail
11 · Integration

Ecosystem integration

Apex shares one API with Apex POS and Charlie HRMS — but you stay in control of what reaches your books.

  • Passport-scoped, idempotent REST endpoints
  • POS Z-readings land in a staging inbox — you choose what to import
  • Each import becomes a draft journal entry for review
  • HRMS payroll summaries mapped server-side
12 · Governance

Companies, roles & audit

Run many companies from one login, each with its own users, roles and books — and a permanent audit trail.

  • Multi-company tenancy; data is company-scoped
  • Per-company roles (Owner, Accountant, Approver, Bookkeeper, Viewer)
  • Immutable audit log & login events
  • Full company data export
02 — How-to guide

Doing the work

Short, task-oriented walkthroughs for the everyday jobs. Each follows the demo company, Dari Ventures Corp.

START HERE

Sign in & choose your company

  1. Open /admin in your browser.
  2. Enter your email and password and click Sign in.
  3. If you belong to more than one company, pick one from the company switcher at the top-left.
  4. You land on the dashboard — cash position, AR, AP and running output VAT.
Tip: roles are scoped per company. The same person can be an Accountant in one company and a Viewer in another.
RECEIVABLES

Create & post a sales invoice

  1. Go to Invoices and click New invoice.
  2. Pick the customer, the invoice date, and the pricing mode (VAT-inclusive or exclusive).
  3. Add line items — item or description, quantity, unit price, tax code and income account.
  4. Click Create. Apex builds the entry — Dr A/R · Cr Sales · Cr Output VAT — and posts it.
With approval enabled, the invoice posts as a draft awaiting an approver instead of hitting the ledger immediately.
PAYABLES

Enter a vendor bill with VAT & withholding

  1. Go to Bills → New bill; choose the vendor and date.
  2. Add expense or asset lines. For each, set the input-VAT bucket: direct-vatable, exempt, or common.
  3. Apply a withholding code if the vendor is subject to EWT.
  4. Create to post Dr expense · Dr Input VAT · Cr A/P.
Common input VAT lands in a deferred account and is allocated quarterly — the BIR way.
CASH

Receive a customer payment

  1. Open Receive Payments and start a new receipt.
  2. Choose the customer and the deposit account.
  3. Allocate the amount across one or more open invoices.
  4. Save — cash is debited, A/R cleared, and invoice statuses update automatically.
INTEGRATION

Import POS Z-readings

  1. Apex POS pushes each day's Z-reading to the API; it appears under Integrations → POS Z-Readings as pending.
  2. Review the figures — sales by VAT class, output VAT, discounts and tender mix.
  3. Tick the readings to keep and click Import as draft (or Import selected in bulk).
  4. Each becomes a draft journal entry; review and post it like any other.
Why staged? Apex is a separate system from POS — like any external accounting software, nothing reaches your books until you choose to import it. Use Dismiss to ignore a reading.
BANKING

Reconcile a bank account

  1. Under Bank Statements, Import CSV for the account.
  2. Use Auto-match to link lines to existing ledger entries, or Post to ledger with a contra account.
  3. Start a Reconciliation, tick the cleared lines, and confirm the statement balance.
  4. Apex closes the reconciliation only when the difference is 0.00.
REPORTS

Run & export financial reports

  1. Open any report under Reports — say Trial Balance.
  2. Set the As of date (or the range, for P&L and cash flow).
  3. Read on screen; figures tie back to the posted ledger.
  4. Click Export XLSX or Export PDF to file or share.
BIR

Prepare a BIR tax return

  1. Go to Tax Returns and choose a form (e.g. 2550Q).
  2. Select the period; Apex computes the figures from posted transactions.
  3. File to persist an immutable snapshot of the return.
  4. Download the PDF facsimile and any alphalist / SLSP exports.
ADMIN

Invite users & assign roles

  1. Open Team (Settings).
  2. Add a person by email and pick their role in this company.
  3. Permissions follow the role — from full Owner access down to read-only Viewer.
  4. Every posting and login is recorded in the Audit Log.

Owner

Full access, company settings, users & export.

Accountant

The full posting workflow plus reports & audit.

Approver

Approve & post; manage periods & accounts.

Bookkeeper

Draft & submit documents — no posting.

Viewer

Read-only access to reports.

03 — Reference

Integration API

Passport-authenticated, scoped, idempotent. Base path /api/v1. Each ecosystem client gets only the scopes it needs.

MethodEndpointScopePurpose
POST/journal-entriesje:postPost a balanced manual journal entry
POST/invoicesinvoice:postIssue a sales invoice
POST/pos/z-readingspos:postStage a POS day-end Z-reading for import
POST/hrms/payrollhrms:postMap a payroll summary to a journal entry
GET/accountsreports:readList the chart of accounts
GET/reports/trial-balancereports:readTrial balance as of a date
All write endpoints honour an Idempotency-Key header — replaying a request returns the original result instead of double-posting.
